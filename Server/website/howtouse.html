How to use FamilyParking?




1. Register yourself inserting your username and email.

2 Insert the code that FamilyParking have sent to your email. Well you are registered!

3 Add new car or new group push on + button and select New Car or New Group.

4 Save your car position, you have to push on P button when you parked your car.

To know where your car is parked you have only to open FamilyParking !
